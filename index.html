FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our treatments, insurance, and what to expect.

Deep TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) uses targeted magnetic pulses to stimulate nerve cells deep within the brain. It's FDA-approved for depression and OCD, completely non-invasive, requires no anesthesia, and each session takes about 20 minutes. Most patients resume normal activities immediately after.

Yes, most major insurance providers cover TMS therapy, including Medicare, Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, and United Healthcare. Our team handles insurance verification and pre-authorization so you can focus on your treatment.

A standard Deep TMS course is 36 sessions over 6 weeks (5 days per week). We also offer an accelerated 6-day protocol for select patients. Your psychiatrist will create a personalized treatment plan based on your specific condition and response.

SPRAVATO (esketamine) is an FDA-approved nasal spray specifically designed for treatment-resistant depression. It's administered in our clinic under medical supervision and can provide rapid relief, often within hours to days, when traditional antidepressants haven't been effective.

TMS has no systemic side effects — no weight gain, no sexual dysfunction, no drowsiness. The most common side effect is mild scalp discomfort during the first few sessions, which typically resolves quickly. You can drive yourself to and from appointments.

Simply schedule a free consultation through our website or call (949) 996-5355. During your initial visit, our psychiatrist will evaluate your condition, review your TMS candidacy, and build your personalized Deep TMS protocol — so you can start treatment quickly.
